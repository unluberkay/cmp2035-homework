What is IBC®

The IBC® Conference is an unrivalled global destination form discussion and debate about the many different challenges facing the electronic media and entertainment industry, both in its sessions and in the range of networking opportunities it affords. Featuring some of the foremost thought-leaders, innovators and policy makers in their fields and covering a wide breadth of topics, the IBC® 2015 Conference was the place to explore new strategies, understand business disruptors, chart future technological progress and uncover the future roadmap of the industry.
